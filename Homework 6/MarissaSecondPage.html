Short Story


This is a story about my sister and her husband, and how they ended up with 2 beautiful miracle babies.

My sister is 8 years older than I, and has always been a huge role model in my life. She was married in 2008, I was in 8th grade.

My sister and her husband tried for kids, for many years but were told by many doctors, their chances weren't high.

They never fully lost faith, but were close at times. In 2019, my brother in law's dad was diagnosed with cancer, that rapidly

took over his body. Over the next year, before he passed, he prayed to God everyday that my sister and brother would have a child.

With everyone gathering and focusing on the health of her father in law, my sister had started to let go of the possibility of

having a baby.

Come July 2020, my brother in law's dad passed. We had the memorial in October, and just a few days after that my

sister found out she was pregnant. When they announced to the family, they were expecting, my sister found a onesie that read:

"Hand picked for Earth, by my Grandpa in Heaven." The doctors monitored my sister's pregnancy because she was diagnosed with

gestational diabetes as well.

June 2021, my sister had her first baby girl, healthy and strong, and so beautiful. Everyone was so excited for the new addition.

To our families surprise, my sister announced in Aug 2022, at my sister and brother in law's anniversary, they were expecting

baby girl #2.

March 2023, my sister had her second baby girl, just as precious as her first.

I knew I wanted to do something special and unique for my 2 baby nieces that they could keep for a long time. So I started

drawing and working on an art piece for each of them.

I gifted the final pieces to my sister and brother in law on Christmas of 2023. I love how they turned out and I know they will

enjoy them as they grow. They are name plaques for either their bedroom doors or to hang up in their rooms.
